Analytic Combinatorics,  Modular  Forms,   and  Number  Theory  REU

June 23 - August 1, 2025.

The 2025 USC Number Theory REU is a six-week, in-person research experience for undergraduates, held in Columbia, SC (Cola City), and supported by the NSA grant. Six participants will work in two groups of three, exploring topics in analytic combinatorics, modular forms, and number theory. Research areas will include problems in combinatorial and number-theoretic sequences, modular form congruences, integer partitions, and related topics.

Each summer, 2~3 plenary speakers will be invited to share insights from their research and their experiences with REU programs. The program also features enrichment activities and career development opportunities.

The program will run from June 23 to August 1, 2025, and provides housing, meals, and a generous living stipend. Preference will be given to applicants from local and regional institutions and to those who have not previously had research opportunities, though all applications are welcome. U.S. citizenship or permanent residency is required.
